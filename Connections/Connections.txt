🔌 Circuit Connections
ESP32 → Relay Module
| ESP32 Pin | Relay Pin |
| --------- | --------- |
| VIN (5V)  | VCC       |
| GND       | GND       |
| GPIO 26   | IN        |
⚠ Do not connect anything to COM/NO unless connecting a load.

🖼 Relay Pin Layout
VCC → Power
GND → Ground
IN → Control pin
COM → Common
NO → Normally Open
NC → Normally Closed
